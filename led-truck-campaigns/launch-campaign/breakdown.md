# Mars Outdoor - LED Truck Launch Campaign

## Project Overview
This campaign showcases cutting-edge mobile LED advertising technology through cinematic 3D renders and AI-enhanced visuals. The project demonstrates Mars Outdoor's innovative LED truck solutions for outdoor advertising, combining photorealistic 3D modeling with AI-generated creative content.

## Role & Responsibilities
- **Creative Director & 3D Artist**: Conceptualized and executed the complete visual direction
- **3D Modeling & Rendering**: Created photorealistic LED truck models and environments
- **AI Video Generation**: Produced dynamic promotional content using AI tools
- **Post-Production**: Enhanced and refined renders for professional presentation

## Tools & Software Used
- **3D Modeling**: Blender 3D (modeling, texturing, lighting, rendering)
- **AI Generation**: Gemini AI, Runway Gen-3, LTX Studio
- **Post-Production**: Adobe Photoshop, Premiere Pro
- **Rendering Engine**: Cycles/Eevee for photorealistic output

## Creative Process

### 1. Concept Development
- Researched modern LED truck advertising trends
- Developed visual style focused on cinematic realism and urban environments
- Created mood boards and reference materials

### 2. 3D Production Pipeline
- **Modeling**: Built detailed LED truck geometry with accurate proportions
- **Texturing**: Applied PBR materials for realistic surfaces
- **Lighting**: Designed dramatic night-time urban lighting scenarios
- **Rendering**: Produced multiple camera angles and variations

### 3. AI Enhancement
- Generated complementary visuals using Gemini AI
- Created dynamic video content with Runway Gen-3
- Refined AI outputs to match brand aesthetic

### 4. Final Delivery
- Rendered high-resolution images (1024x540 and higher)
- Optimized web formats (.webp) for portfolio presentation
- Created multiple variations for different use cases

## Key Highlights
- **Photorealistic Quality**: Achieved cinema-grade realism in 3D renders
- **Urban Integration**: Seamlessly placed LED trucks in realistic city environments
- **Dynamic Content**: Combined static renders with AI-generated motion content
- **Professional Presentation**: Delivered portfolio-ready assets

## Technical Specifications
- **Resolution**: 1024x540px (web-optimized), source files up to 4K
- **Format**: WebP for web, PNG/JPEG for print
- **Render Time**: Approximately 2-5 minutes per frame (depending on complexity)
- **Software Versions**: Blender 4.x, Runway Gen-3, Gemini AI
